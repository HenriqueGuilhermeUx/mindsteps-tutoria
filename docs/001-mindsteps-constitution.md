# MindSteps Constitution

Version: 1.0 (Draft)

## Purpose

The MindSteps Constitution defines the principles that guide every architectural, pedagogical and business decision of the platform.

## Article 0 — Why We Exist

We believe every child deserves the opportunity to discover how they learn best.

## Article 1 — The Learner Comes First

The student is the center of every decision. Technology, AI, interfaces and business models exist to support learning.

## Article 2 — AI Serves Learning

Artificial Intelligence must promote autonomy, curiosity and critical thinking. It must never replace reasoning.

## Article 3 — Families and Teachers

MindSteps strengthens families and teachers. It does not replace them.

## Article 4 — Universal Access

There will always be a free version of MindSteps. Commercial success should expand access, not restrict it.

## Article 5 — Ethics

Child safety, privacy and transparency are non-negotiable.

## Article 6 — Learning Before Engagement

Success is measured by meaningful learning, not screen time.

## Article 7 — Scientific Foundation

Important product decisions should be supported by learning science whenever possible.

## Article 8 — Long-term Vision

Every important decision should still make sense ten years from now.

## Final Commitment

We are building a platform that helps people discover how they learn best while making personalized learning accessible to as many children as possible.
